# AI Risk Management Framework

A unified, standards-based framework for managing AI risks by aligning NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, and MITRE ATLAS.

## What it Does

Standardizes AI risk identification, assessment, and mitigation.

Reduces compliance and control redundancy.

Improves security, transparency, and governance.

## How

Control and risk mapping across standards.


## Goal

Enable secure, compliant, and responsible AI deployment without slowing innovation.
